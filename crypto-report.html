立法动态 Legislative Update

美国立法全面提速 / U.S. Crypto Legislation Accelerating

2026年，美国国会在加密货币监管立法上迈出历史性步伐，两项关键法案正在重塑市场格局。 In 2026, the U.S. Congress is taking historic steps in crypto regulation, with two landmark bills reshaping the market landscape.

CLARITY Act
数字资产市场结构法案 Digital Asset Market Structure Act
⏳ 参议院审议中 ⏳ Senate Review
  • 众议院以294-134高票通过 House passed 294-134
  • 56%概率在2026年底前由总统签署成法 56% probability of presidential signature by end of 2026
  • 将以下资产明确定性为数字商品： Classifies the following as digital commodities:
₿ BTC Ξ ETH ◎ SOL ✕ XRP
⚠️ Senator Lummis： "若参议院不加速推进，立法可能延误4年。" "If the Senate doesn't accelerate, legislation could be delayed 4 years."
🔗 来源：ReutersSource: Reuters
GENIUS Act
稳定币监管法案 Stablecoin Regulatory Act
✅ 已签署Signed
  • 美国首个稳定币联邦监管框架正式确立 Establishes America's first federal stablecoin regulatory framework
  • 已由Trump总统正式签署，即刻生效 Formally signed into law by President Trump, effective immediately
  • 为稳定币发行方设立明确合规路径 Creates clear compliance pathways for stablecoin issuers
  • 消除数字美元领域法律不确定性 Eliminates legal uncertainty in the digital dollar space
  • 推动美元稳定币全球竞争力 Enhances global competitiveness of USD stablecoins
🏛️ 历史意义🏛️ Historical Significance
这是美国有史以来首部专门针对加密货币的联邦法律，标志着加密立法时代正式开启。 This is America's first-ever dedicated federal crypto law, marking the formal dawn of the crypto legislative era.
