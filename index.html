The Problem

Creators build audiences.
Platforms own the data.

The creator economy is broken. Billions of creators generate immense value — yet the platforms harvest, own, and monetize that data without sharing meaningful control.

🔒

Data Locked Inside Platforms

Your audience data — follower behavior, engagement patterns, fan relationships — lives entirely on platforms you don't control. One algorithm change can erase years of work overnight.

💸

Influence Without Ownership

Creators build massive influence and trust, but can't collateralize it, transfer it, or prove its value to brands. Your biggest asset has no deed, no market, no liquidity.

🏭

AI Commoditizing Content

As AI floods every feed with infinite content, raw content creation becomes worthless. What remains scarce — and therefore priceless — is verified human attention and trust.

The Solution

Your influence.
Your data. Your asset.

Xavvi introduces TDA — Trustable Data Asset — a new paradigm that transforms the bond between creators and their fans into verifiable, on-chain data assets. Think of it as Data Real Estate.

🏗️

Capture & Structure

We capture creator-fan interaction data across platforms and structure it into standardized, portable data assets you actually own.

🔐

Verify & Certify

Web3 technology anchors each TDA to its creator with cryptographic proof — making trust machine-verifiable and tamper-proof.

📈

Monetize & Trade

TDAs become tradable assets in a transparent creator marketplace — enabling creators to license, collateralize, and grow their influence equity.

♾️

Compound & Grow

As your audience deepens, your TDA appreciates — like real estate. Your influence becomes a compounding, generational asset.
